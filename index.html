About Me

I am a results-driven Business Course Sales Representative with 20 years of hands-on experience in business course acquisition, customer onboarding, and relationship management. My career has been defined by one consistent mission: connecting the right business owners with the right knowledge.

I specialize in identifying potential new business owners, understanding their unique needs, and matching them with the ideal AI-powered business solutions.
